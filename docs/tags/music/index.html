All About Dat Brass

June 07, 2017
I emerged from the gymnasium into the cool night air. I had just completed another evening’s instruction in the Eastern martial arts, and was feeling invigorated, it being a weekly ritual before my jaunt from London Bridge to my rooms in Vauxhaul. Having attained the pavement I became swept up along with the rest of the commutors flowing to and from the station. I was bound away from this hive of activity, towards my favourite part of town: the borough of Southwark.
